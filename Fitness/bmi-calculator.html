BMI Categories

BMI is a measurement of a person's weight with respect to their height. It's a good general indicator of healthy weight, but does not account for muscle mass, bone density, and other factors.

Underweight

BMI less than 18.5

Normal Weight

BMI 18.5 to 24.9

Overweight

BMI 25 to 29.9

Obese

BMI 30 or greater
